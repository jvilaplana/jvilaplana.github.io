Is possible the Segmentation? Why?

  • No
  • The memory space of each segment must be contiguous
  • ProcB needs 5 pages (2560 B / 512 B)
  • DataL and the stack need 2 pages (1024 B / 512 B)
  • We only have 1 contiguous page (all pairs are already taken)
